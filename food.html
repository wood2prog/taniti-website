Taniti

Food

Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Jump to grocery stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Island Bounty Seafood Restaurant

At Island Bounty Seafood Restaurant, we bring the ocean to your plate with the freshest seafood caught daily and prepared to perfection. Nestled in a cozy coastal setting, our menu features everything from succulent lobster and buttery crab legs to expertly grilled fish and a variety of island-inspired dishes. Each meal is crafted with care, combining local flavors with culinary artistry. Whether you're indulging in our signature seafood platter or sipping on a tropical cocktail while enjoying the ocean breeze, Island Bounty is your ultimate destination for a taste of paradise. Perfect for family dinners, romantic evenings, or casual gatherings by the sea!

Grocery Stores

Island Source

Discover the freshest local produce, premium meats, and pantry staples at Island Source, your one-stop grocery store for island-inspired ingredients and flavors. With a wide selection of organic fruits, vegetables, and sustainably sourced products, Island Source brings the best of the tropics to your kitchen. Whether you’re stocking up on everyday essentials or seeking specialty items for your next culinary adventure, our shelves are filled with quality and variety. Friendly service, a clean environment, and an emphasis on supporting local farmers and vendors make Island Source the perfect place to shop for your island lifestyle.

Menaia & Sons Grocery

Step into Menaia & Sons Grocery, a family-owned market that’s been serving the community for generations. Known for its personalized service and high-quality selection, we offer a range of fresh produce, premium meats, locally sourced dairy, and everyday grocery essentials. Whether you're shopping for a week's worth of meals or seeking a specialty ingredient, you'll find everything you need here, all at great prices. With a legacy of trust and a commitment to quality, Menaia & Sons Grocery is more than just a store—it’s a part of the neighborhood. Come experience the warmth of a family business that puts you first.

Taniti Gas & Convenience Groceries

At Taniti Gas & Convenience Groceries, we make it easy to fuel up and stock up all in one stop. Whether you’re on the go or just need to pick up a few essentials, we offer a wide variety of snacks, beverages, household items, and fresh groceries at competitive prices. Our store is your go-to for quick, convenient shopping with a friendly smile, offering everything from fresh bread and dairy to ready-to-eat meals and last-minute necessities. Stop by for gas, grab your groceries, and get back on the road with ease at Taniti Gas & Convenience Groceries!
